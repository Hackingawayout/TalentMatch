AI Chatbot Development

This project involves developing an advanced AI chatbot for customer service automation. Participants will be working on the following key areas:

  • Designing conversational flows for a seamless user experience.
  • Implementing and integrating Natural Language Processing (NLP) algorithms.
  • Testing the chatbot in real-world customer service scenarios.
  • Deploying the chatbot to a scalable platform.

This is an excellent opportunity for developers, UI/UX designers, and AI enthusiasts to collaborate on an innovative project. The goal is to create a chatbot capable of handling complex customer queries, improving business efficiency, and ensuring customer satisfaction.

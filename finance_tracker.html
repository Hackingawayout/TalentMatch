Personal Finance Tracker

This project focuses on developing a comprehensive personal finance tracking tool to help users manage their income, expenses, and savings efficiently. Participants will work on the following core functionalities:

  • Budget tracking and expense categorization.
  • Visual insights with graphs and charts for income vs. expenses.
  • Goal setting for savings and spending limits.
  • Integration with bank APIs for real-time transaction updates.

This project is perfect for developers, data enthusiasts, and designers who aim to build solutions for better financial literacy. The goal is to provide users with an intuitive platform to achieve financial stability and meet their goals.

Travel Planner

This project is about creating a user-friendly travel planning tool to simplify trip organization and make travel hassle-free. Participants will work on the following key features:

  • Itinerary creation with customizable day-wise plans.
  • Integration of flight, hotel, and activity bookings.
  • Interactive maps to explore destinations and attractions.
  • Expense tracking for trip budgeting.

This project is ideal for travel enthusiasts, developers, and UI/UX designers. The goal is to offer a seamless platform that enhances the travel planning experience and ensures stress-free trips.

E-commerce Platform Development

This project focuses on building a dynamic and feature-rich e-commerce platform to enable online buying and selling. Participants will work on the following core features:

  • Designing a visually appealing and user-friendly interface for browsing products.
  • Implementing a secure and scalable shopping cart system.
  • Integrating multiple payment gateways for seamless transactions.
  • Developing a robust backend for inventory management and order tracking.

This project is perfect for front-end developers, back-end engineers, and UI/UX designers who want to explore the exciting world of e-commerce. The goal is to create a platform that enhances customer experience while ensuring business scalability.
